Take Action

The humanitarian response towards helping those affected by Pakistan’s floods is being led by the Pakistani government. With assistance from United Nations agencies, other humanitarian organizations, and donors like yourself, assistance is being provided to flood-affected communities.

Some critical needs of these flood-affected districts include:

  • Food security
  • Shelter
  • Healthcare
  • Education support
  • Cash assistance
